Blur the line between learn and play

At Codie we believe that coding is an essential knowledge that is needed to prepare children for the future. We are working on an intuitive programming language for touch screen devices. With thist application you can create the brain of the robot that we are developing as well.

Meet Codie

Our handmade wooden toy robot dedicated to inspire the next generation through teaching creative coding. We would like to ensure everything that is required for a great programmable robotic toy to teach kids the principles of coding and show them how to use technology as flexible as they can.
