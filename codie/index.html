Should everyone be a programmer?

No. Instead we believe that everyone should be digital literate. Technology issurrounding us within the heart of every electronic device. Even when using an Excel sheet, you are doing programming. It is an essential skill for everyone to know the basics of programming, the algorithmic thinking.

Blur the line between learn and play

Codie is all about the education of algorithmic thinking. While playing with Codie kids not only enjoy the time spent with it, but they learn the basics of coding.
Using our brand new graphical programming language, they can really understand how computers think.
